Executive Summary

The graph below has average earnings to price on the y-axis and average percentage lagged change in earnings on the x-axis. Percentage lagged change in earnings ranges from 0.9 to 1.43 while average earnings to price ratio ranges from -188 to 81.

Text

Earnings and price data for North American companies were gathered from Compustat. Then, the three-year average percentage lagged change in earnings and average earnings to price ratio were calculated for each company. Then the average percentage lagged change in earnings and average earnings to price ratio data were truncated at 10th and 90th quantiles. Then the data was plotted, x-axis is average percentage lagged change in earnings and y-axis is average earnings to price ratio.

raw_data <- read.csv("corpfin_hw6.csv")
data_work <- raw_data[,c(1,3,12,14)]
data_work$ep_ratio <- data_work$re/data_work$prcc_f
calc_avg_change <- function(a){
  R_avg=((((a[2]/a[1])+1)*((a[3]/a[2])+1))^(1/2))-1
  
  return(R_avg)
}
calculate_result <- data_work %>% 
  group_by(gvkey) %>%
  summarize(earning_growth=calc_avg_change(re),
    avg_ep_ratio=mean(ep_ratio,na.rm=TRUE))
calculate_result <- na.omit(calculate_result)
limit_earning_growth <- quantile(calculate_result$earning_growth,probs=c(0.1,0.9))
limit_avg_ep_ratio<- quantile(calculate_result$avg_ep_ratio,probs=c(0.1,0.9))
calculate_result <- calculate_result[which(calculate_result$earning_growth<limit_earning_growth[2]&calculate_result$earning_growth>limit_earning_growth[1]),]
calculate_result <- calculate_result[which(calculate_result$avg_ep_ratio<limit_avg_ep_ratio[2]&calculate_result$avg_ep_ratio>limit_avg_ep_ratio[1]),]
plot(x=calculate_result$earning_growth,y=calculate_result$avg_ep_ratio,xlab="earnings change",ylab="E/P ratio",pch=20,col="blue")
